Here, at GloAfrica GLO Africa, an abbreviation of “Global Leaders Organization Africa” is a Non-Governmental Organization that networks young leaders and professionals across Africa. GLO Africa exists to connect Africa’s young and great minds willing enough to come together, work together and solve the problems of their communities.

Our Services

Our service range from Youth empowerment To Leadership Trainings and Global Networking

Glo Africa seeks to create a community of thoughtful, committed, young people, and to engage them in series of conversations to ignite a fire in their hearts stirring them passionately to thrive and become agents of change.
